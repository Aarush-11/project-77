Console screen is very unique. You can use a console screen to have fun with or to just learn about it. To open the Console Screen go to the tab you want and click inspect. On the top click Console and you are done. To print something on the Console Screen you go to html, and then make a variable to store the value. Then do console.log and in the round brackets after put the variable you want to print.
